FROM SIGNUP TO UNFAIR ADVANTAGE IN 5 MINUTES

No sales calls. No training sessions. No 90-day implementation. Just tell us what you care about and start winning.

01
MINUTE 1

Sign up. Tell us your industry. We do the rest.

Create your firm account. Our AI asks what you do — restaurants, housing, healthcare, energy — and instantly surfaces every active California bill that matters to you. One click and they're all tracked. Invite your team with a link.

Switching from CapitolTrack?

Upload your CSV. We auto-match your clients, bills, positions, and notes. Your entire history migrates in minutes — not months.

02
EVERY DAY, AUTOMATICALLY

50,000+ bills. Always current. Always searchable.

Our pipeline syncs with the California Legislature daily — every bill, amendment, vote, hearing, and committee action back to 1999. Search in plain English. The AI understands "bills that affect restaurant labor costs" — not just keywords.

Plus 150+ local jurisdictions

City councils and county boards across California, tracked alongside state bills. Regulatory actions and budget trailer bills too.

03
YOUR DAILY WORKFLOW

Manage clients. Set positions. Prep for meetings.

Track support/oppose/watch across every client. Log every meeting, call, and email with legislators in the stakeholder CRM. Before your next meeting, one click generates a prep package: legislator bio, voting history on your bills, prior firm interactions, and AI-generated talking points.

Relationship gap alerts

Two weeks before a hearing, Legisly flags committee members your firm hasn't contacted in 90 days. You walk in prepared. Always.

04
IN SECONDS

AI drafts your letters. Your testimony. Your amendments.

Need a position letter? Tell the AI "draft an oppose letter for AB-1234 for my restaurant client" and it's done — in proper CA legislative format, addressed to the right committee. Same for committee testimony, amendment language, and meeting request emails.

Passage prediction

Every bill gets a likelihood-of-passage score based on sponsor strength, committee composition, and 25 years of historical data. Know where to spend your energy.

05
REAL-TIME

Live hearing intelligence. No other platform has this.

When a committee hearing goes live, Legisly captures the audio, transcribes it in real-time, identifies speakers, and runs AI analysis on every word. Votes detected via OCR within 30 seconds. Support and oppose testimony classified automatically. Your phone buzzes before the hearing is even over.

After the gavel drops

AI-generated hearing summary in 15 minutes. Video playback synced to the searchable transcript. Click any line to jump to that exact moment. Share with your team or your clients instantly.

06
FOR YOUR CLIENTS

Give them a portal they'll actually use.

Every client gets their own branded, live dashboard — your firm's logo, your colors, real-time bill status, hearing alerts, and position notes. Clients can comment directly on bills and submit recommendations. No more emailing PDFs that nobody reads.

Branded reports on autopilot

Professional reports with cover pages, your branding, and a table of contents — generated and emailed to clients on whatever schedule you set. Weekly, monthly, or after every major hearing.
