THE CAPITOL DOESN'T WAIT. NEITHER DO WE.

Legisly is legislative intelligence built by people who've worked the Capitol — for everyone with something at stake.

Sacramento moves at the speed of politics. Thousands of bills. Hundreds of hearings. Votes that reshape entire industries — sometimes in a single afternoon.

Your current tools weren't built for this. They were built in 1993.

WHAT WE BUILT

A platform that sees everything happening in the California Legislature — in real time — and tells you what matters before anyone else knows.

  • Live hearing intelligence. Real-time transcription, AI analysis, and vote detection while hearings are still happening.
  • AI that does the work. Summaries, position letters, testimony, amendments, meeting prep — generated in seconds.
  • A client portal that replaces PDFs. Live, branded, interactive dashboards your clients actually want to use.
  • 150+ local jurisdictions. City councils and county boards across California, tracked alongside state legislation.
  • One-click migration. Leave your legacy platform behind. Bring your clients, positions, and history with you.

WHY WE BUILT IT

Because legislative intelligence shouldn't cost $30,000 a year. Because small businesses deserve the same visibility as the biggest firms. Because the tools lobbyists rely on haven't changed in three decades — and the Capitol has.

We built Legisly to democratize access to the legislative process in California. Not to replace lobbyists — to make every lobbyist, every government affairs team, and every advocacy organization more effective.
